Last Call – Narrative Puzzle Adventure

Explore an abandoned house in the middle of a forest.
Receive a mysterious call for help from your missing friend.
Uncover hidden clues and secrets to solve the mystery.

About This Project

In this prototype game, the player steps into a tense, mysterious atmosphere as they visit an abandoned house deep inside a forest.
After receiving a phone call from their missing friend, they must explore the creepy surroundings to uncover secrets piece by piece.
Players collect strange symbols, mysterious clues, hidden maps, and useful tools by solving card-based puzzles, all connected to the narrative.
The goal is to solve the mystery of the abandoned house by strategically collecting sets of clues while piecing together what happened.
Each decision brings players closer to discovering the dark secret or failing to escape in time.
