Neon Labs: Multiplayer Sci-Fi Deathmatch

A futuristic sci-fi multiplayer deathmatch arena. Players fight using advanced energy weapons in a high-tech lab. Fast-paced action with strategic map design and survival mechanics.

About This Project

Neon Labs: Multiplayer Sci-Fi Deathmatch is a competitive first-person shooter set in a futuristic science laboratory.

Players engage in fast-paced deathmatches, armed with advanced energy-based weapons and limited resources. The environment is designed as an advanced research facility featuring neon-lit corridors, experimental chambers, and tactical vantage points. With ammo scarcity, health management, and dynamic map layouts, players must balance aggressive combat with survival strategy.

This project demonstrates:
• Multiplayer Gameplay Systems (lobby, player connections, deathmatch rules).
• Combat Mechanics (shooting, reloading, health and ammo systems).
• Immersive Level Design (sci-fi lab aesthetics, lighting, and atmosphere).
• Player Experience (UI for ammo, health, and score; smooth controls; tactical engagement).
