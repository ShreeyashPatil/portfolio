Meteor Defender – Simple Space Fighter Game

Destroy meteors and protect your spacecraft.
Earn a high score while avoiding damage.
Simple 2D animation game with basic controls.

About This Project

This was my first ever game project, where you play as a space fighter pilot facing a constant meteor rain.
The player’s goal is to destroy incoming meteors by shooting them, earning points for each destroyed meteor.
There is no specific ending or story progression—just survive as long as possible.
The player has a limited HP bar that decreases every time a meteor hits the spacecraft.
If the HP reaches zero, the game ends, and the player’s score is saved in the top scores list.
The game is made with simple 2D animations, basic controls, and a focus on straightforward fun and challenge.
It’s not fancy, but it taught me the basics of game design and programming, especially handling animations, scoring systems, and player interactions.
