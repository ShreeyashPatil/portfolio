Stickman Warriors – 2D Multiplayer Battle Game

1v1 battles with different weapons like swords and axes.
Choose from multiple stickman characters with unique abilities.
Simple, fun, and competitive multiplayer action.

About This Project

Stickman Warriors is a 2D multiplayer game where players face off in 1v1 battles using a variety of weapons, such as swords, axes, and other melee tools.
Players can select from different stickman characters, each with their own proficiency level and abilities, adding strategy to the match.
The game focuses on fast-paced action with simple controls, making it easy to pick up but challenging to master.
The goal is to defeat the opponent by using weapons skillfully and reacting quickly.
It’s designed for casual fun, but also encourages competitive play by allowing players to experiment with different characters and tactics.
Although simple, it helped me learn about player balancing, weapon mechanics, and multiplayer logic.
